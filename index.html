Guinea Pigs?

Guinea Pigs, for the uninitiated are vegetable compactors which lack traditional hydraulic mechanisms as well as tails. Guinea pigs are not from papua new guinea and are from a cage in my livingroom. The optimal number of pigs is 2, ask anybody. Guinea Pigs also have talons not unlike birds of prey (which tend to also lack tails). Guinea Pigs make for better pets than most other animals with talons, and even have the ability to clean themselves. This allows for Guinea Pigs to go much longer than the average vehicle without a wash. Owners of geepee's should provide a healthy diet of consturctive criticism, positive reinforcment, and those treats that they like even though they are kind of expensive.

Caring for Guinea Pigs

3 Dimensional Pigs

Guinea Pigs need space, they're sensitive okay? They also need at least one friend so that the can perform ceremonial pig combat over resources. A pair of pigs should have somewhere between 9 square feet and an aircraft carrier of space. If you do put guinea pigs on an aircraft carrier at least make them wear little sailor hats, like, please. I built my enclosure out of cube storage racks and choroplast, which you can buy at any home depot--and struggle to fit into your corolla at any home depot parking lot. All this space adds up to at least 30% of your house dedicated to guinea pigs, plus 75% of your refrigerator.

Romaine, So Much Romaine

Guinea pigs eat a LOT... like, a lot. I go to Vons like twice a week just so they can go hog wild on the daily. The pigs like romaine lettuce, roma tomatoes, cucumber, bell pepper, and carros. Cowpig in particular mows down that seeded white part at the middle of the bell pepper, she goes ham on that thing. Other than the veggies, pigs ought to be fed BALES OF HAY. Yea, like a horse would eat. Timothy hay in unholy quantities needs to be pumped into the pig cage daily. For an OPTIMAL PIG, something like 70% of its diet should be hay. There's also the pellets, they're kind of expensive but they give pigs vitamin C, which they dont make themselves for some reason, and the pigs like 'em, too. If those pigs been good, you can also give them treats if you like. Pig treats are usually compressed hay with stuff in it, but the pigs love the sound of rustling bags and they seem to like treats too. It also doesnt hurt that guinea pigs eat people food, so you can use food stamps to buy food for your pigs. Welfare pigs 💸.

Vet Bills:
(╯°□°）╯︵ ┻━┻

Guinea pigs are very exotic, like a fiat (the most exotic, guinea-pig shaped car). unlike a fiat, or a girlfriend, you should weigh your guinea pigs often, this can help you spot if there are any health issues under the hood. If you suspect an issue, you can't just take your pig to a normal vet, either. Guinea pigs are exotic animals and thus require exotics vets, which cost more. My Cowpig once poked herself in the eye while trying to Scrooge McDuck a pile of fresh timothy hay. My exotics vet recommended that "if it has anything to do with their eyes or their balls, don't wait." Now that's an exotic vet.
